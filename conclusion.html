The Campies

Before we get to the grand winners ceremony, we will begin our yearly tradition, the Campie awards featuring personal trophies:

Final Results

Before the final cabin results, here are the top 10 scoring campers this year!

And now, without further ado..... Here are the cabin rankings for Smash Camp 2021!

The Golden Girls: 3721

In fourth place, we have The Golden Girls. Veteran counselor Kat rallied troops from as early as pre-camp, fostering a positive and collaborative environment. Members Bulbajohn, Cat, and Coal Soul did their best to earn points for their cabin and showed they were a real contender up until the last day. While The Golden Girls may have finished last, they embodied the true spirit of Betty White by spreading joy everywhere they went.

Sigma Squadron: 3990

In third place, we have Sigma Squadron. First time counselor Atlus and his sigma attitude had Cabin 4 get off to a great start. Top gamer Pesti had another good year in the scorecards, while cabinmates like Nuuk, Ritvik, and Hot Bread excelled greatly in the other areas, like creative and RPG fight. Sigma Squadron may have tapered off a bit towards the end, but the whole cabin made sure the whole way through that their Sigma presence was felt.

OFFICIAL STEEL SAMURAI FAN CLUB: 4168

In second place, we have OFFICIAL STEEL SAMURAI FAN CLUB. Moxie set the mood in his cabin early, and the whole camp they were straight chillin. Scott, Elmo, and Catball Enthusiast all were consistently present, getting work done and covering for Moxie when he couldn't be there. An additional shoutout to late signee BB, who fit right in and started contributing big immediately. They were just short this year, but in the closest year ever, they could have easily won another year.

DaCabin: 4367

The champion of Smash Camp 2021 is officially DACABIN!!! LESSSS GOOOOO!! Sophomore counselor Big Dan was determined to get the dub this year after just falling short last year, and he got just the group he needed to make it happen. Brian and Calaf carried a lot of weight as well, winning tournaments and contributing to high scores across the board. With this strong trio, Cabin 2's morale was sky high all camp, and they powered through to victory! Congratulations!!

That’s it for this year of Smash Camp! Thank you so much to everyone for joining this year. It was actually the most intense and fun to watch camp in the history of the event. Day Four started with a 26 point difference between first place and fourth place. There was incredible talent, both veteran and brand new. We saw our biggest growth in a very long time with this year and we are so thankful for the new life all of you have brought to this event. We hope you all are as excited for next year and as we are. See you in 2022!
