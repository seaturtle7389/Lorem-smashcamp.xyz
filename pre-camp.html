Pre-Camp Information

The weekend before Smash Camp is considered the pre-camp period. This is where the leaders will sort you into your cabins. It is also the time where you will get to meet new people and get to know your cabin mates. You will come up with your cabin name at this time. Each cabin is run by an individual counselor. Get to know your cabin counselor, as they are there to help you out!

The following events are open for sign-ups during pre-camp:

  • Pokemon Showdown! Tournament (Monotype OU)
  • Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Tournament
  • Chess Tournament
  • Everyone is Mafia*
  • Triva Quiz*

*These events have a limited number of participants and will be chosen by the cabin counselors.

Besides signing up for events before camp starts, this year cabins will also choose their name during pre-camp. Cabin counselors should take suggestions, hold a vote, and submit their name before 11:59 PM EDT on Sunday.

As a general note, even if you have been sorted you may be re-sorted if we deem that it's 100% necessary.
