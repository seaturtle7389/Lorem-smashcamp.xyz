Misc. Events

Just like last year, we will be having some festivities and hangouts happening during camp. Winning during these activities does net you points, but first and foremost, this is mostly for fun. You would get 5 points per win in each activity, for a maximum of 20 . If you reach 20 points, you will not be able to earn more, but please feel free to keep participating. We figure capping it at 20 makes it so people do not try to do this for the points, but still ensures there is a slight incentive for partaking in more activities/events.

NOTE: this means 20 points for each activity. However, do not try to point scum these events cuz it'll be really easy to tell.

  • Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Lobbies
  • Bombparty
