Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Tournament

Yeah you know how it goes, we're doing a Smash Bros. tournament. Dust off your GameCube controller and your Switch and get ready, we're not changing anything from the previous two installments of Smash.

NOTE: Let it be known since Smash Camp is only a week long, you will have to be fairly active each day to consider signing up for this tourney. We donâ€™t want to have to DQ people because they aren't available or whatever so we just wanna make it clear ONLY sign up if you're sure you can be around for at least an hour or two throughout the day every day.

To sign up just post your Switch friend code, your cabin, and timezone in the signup channel.

Example of sign up post:
Switch Friend Code: 4356-3543-5331
Cabin: 5
Timezone and available times: EST (12 PM - 12 AM)

Rules
  • Best of 3, single elimination. A bronze match will be played between semifinals losers. Finals and bronze matches are best of 5.
  • Style: Stock
  • Stock: 3
  • Time Limit: 7:00
  • FS Meter: Off
  • Spirits: Off
  • Items: Off
Please make your ruleset for this tournament right after signing up and double check it.

IMPORTANT: Even if Kazuya releases right after the Sakurai presentation we're still not letting you use him lol

Stage List - Hazards Off
Neutrals
  • Battlefield
  • Final Destination
  • Pokemon Stadium 2
  • Smashville
  • Town and City
Counterpicks
  • Kalos Pokemon League
  • Lylat Cruise
  • Yoshi's Story

Stage selection for the first game - you can do 1/2/1 striking or mutually agree to a stage that isn't a counterpick. After that, the winner bans two stages and the loser picks the next stage from the list of neutrals and counterpicks. If you have any questions about the stage selection procedures or rules in any way, feel free to @ Nick in the Smash Camp Discord

If you don't understand any of what's listed above, just make sure you have the ruleset correct and play all your games on Pokemon Stadium 2

Placements

  • 1st place will be awarded 200 points
  • 2nd place will be awarded 100 points
  • 3rd place will be awarded 50 points
  • 4th place will be awarded 25 points
  • 5th place will be awarded 10 points*
*5th is dependent on how many people sign up and how the bracket plays out.
