TikTok

Create a TikTok-style video! Options ranges from funny skits to speedarts - anything you can think of in 60 seconds or less! Make us laugh or amaze us - it's your choice. The steps are simple: record a thing on your phone, submit it, and hope you get points.

Contest Guidelines

  • The video must be taken vertically, just as if it were in TikTok itself.
  • You don't actually have to face reveal or post this on social media lol.
  • You may use any type of software to edit your video because not everyone is gonna have this app.
  • To submit, upload as a TikTok and post the link OR upload as an unlisted video on YouTube, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc. Provide the link in your submissions channel.
  • There will be no participation points for this event. Failure to follow the time or phone orientation limit will result in disqualification from the leaderboard.
  • No NSFW content or slurs allowed.

  • 1st place will be awarded 100 points
  • 2nd place will be awarded 75 points
  • 3rd place will be awarded 50 points
  • 4th and 5th place will be awarded 25 points
  • 6th through 10th place will be awarded 10 points

RPG Character Fight

Now that your characters have been created, it's time to fight!

Submitting Actions

A counselor and at least 2 other cabin members should have delegation to submit actions. The counselor should tell me which other cabin members can submit actions before the end of Day 1. When it comes time to submit and finalize an action, you will Ping Boss.

You will submit a basic attack or one of your two moves. You will tell me what target you choose for that turn as well. If you use are using an item, you must also say the item you are using along with the target. In the case of a dice roll effecting item, clarify if it will apply to your or an opposing players ability roll or basic attack/move roll.

Scavenger Hunt

A staple of Smash Camp that we're excited to bring back this year! For this event, we will provide a list of items that each camper will need to find and take a picture of in real life. Find them, take a picture of them (with the required proof - see below), and get some points! You will earn 5 points for each picture submitted, with an extra 5 bonus points available for specific items.

Contest Guidelines

  • You may only take photos of these items in person (in your house, at a store, wherever as long as you're physically with the item). It cannot be a screenshot or an image from the internet.
  • Your photo absolutely must be accompanied by a slip of paper with your username and cabin number on it (see the example below).
  • In the past we've been lenient with the validity of submissions that were not totally accurate but showed effort and/or creativity. This year, we will only accept submissions that are accurate as to what we're requesting (e.g. if we ask for a lime, we wouldn't accept a lemon that's been painted green).
  • Bonus points will be available for one item per day. These points will be awarded at the discretion of camp staff for submissions that stand out or are otherwise notable.

Today's items are the following:

  • Toast
  • A media poster (video game, movie, band, etc. - must be legit!)
  • A live animal *Bonus available
